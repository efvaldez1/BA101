REVIEWS

Confirm all the hype regarding B.R.O.

Celeste Ferrer

I've been here two times and nothing has changed. The food here, especially, the beef teriyaki and chicken katsu set are worth it! If you're really hungry and want to eat a lot, this place is perfect. It has a good food, nice ambiance and good service.

Aviva Domasian

The servers and the manager were friendly and attentive. We ordered ebi tempura katsudon and curry pork katsudon, and my gosh, they were delicious! The meat was tender and flavorful. The katsudon tasted even better with a dash of shouyu, which is placed on each table along with other condiments and spices. Serving sizes were quite big; I didn't eat much before our Yabu dinner, but I was barely able to finish my meal. Now I know why B.R.O is so popular, and I'm definitely going back for more!

Joelle

I've heard so many good reviews about this restaurant and was thrilled to finally have had the chance to try it. I absolutely love the interiors and service was great even thou it was not that big. We ordered the california maki, dynamite roll, teriyaki roll, beef teriyaki, and ended the meal with the japanese soda. Overall, I was satisfied with the fusion of flavors of the maki but would have loved it more if the servings were a tad bigger. The beef teriyaki was nice and tender but had too much sauce for my taste. Would definitely come back for the maki.

Zzlleen Lee

I would highly recommend this place to all tempura and gyudon lovers out there. They definitely serve quality food. Ingredients are also fresh and authentic 😍 the gyudon can compare to my favorite gyudon of Oishinbo. At least it's a place closer to QC. Also order the japanese soda!!!
